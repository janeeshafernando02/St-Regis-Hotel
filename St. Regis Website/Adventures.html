Adventure Experiences

One Galle Face Mall

Elevate Your Experience.

High-end shopping and entertainment destination that offers a mix of international and local brands, upscale dining options, a modern cinema complex, and cultural events. Situated in a central location, the mall caters to diverse tastes and preferences, embodying the city's cosmopolitan lifestyle.

Galle Face Green

Nature Meets City Life, Unwind and Thrive

A beautiful stretch of open space sandwiched between the bustling Galle road and the Indian Ocean, Galle Face Green is one of the most popular picnic attractions in Colombo.

Crescat

A mall in the hotel!

Cinnamon Grand is not only the most luxurious business hotel in Colombo, it is also a collective experience for a dynamic traveller. An essential part of this essence is the adjoining shopping mall, Crescat.

Colombo National Museum

Discover ancient Sri Lankan history.

Picture a big white colonial mansion, with a beautifully maintained lawn on the outside and a treasure of ancient Sri Lankan history on the inside. Or, just visit the Colombo National Museum.

Independence Square

Historical landmark in Colombo

Independence Memorial Hall is a national monument in Sri Lanka built for commemoration of the independence of Sri Lanka from the British rule with the restoration of full governing responsibility to a Ceylonese-elected legislature on 4 February 1948.

Port City Colombo

Shaping Tomorrow's Urban Excellence Today.

Port City Sri Lanka is a pioneering development in Colombo, characterized by innovative architecture, sustainability, and advanced infrastructure. It creates a modern urban center with residential, commercial, and recreational spaces, aiming to be a global financial and business hub.

Lotus Tower

Reaching Heights, Connecting Hearts.

One of the most unique structures in Colombo, the Lotus Tower is nearly 350 meters tall and towers over Slave Island. The top of the structure closely resembles the namesake flower. The tower serves as a transmission hub, which aims at improving the quality of transmission communication and broadcast.

Old City Hall

History Speaks Through Architecture.

If you like architecture or history, the Old City Hall on Main Street is amongst the greatest attractions in Colombo. The hall was built nearly 200 years ago, the brilliant architectural vestiges of which era can still be seen in the exteriors.
