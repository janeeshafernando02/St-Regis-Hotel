About Us

St. Regis Group grew out of the vision of one man - Eden Smith - who defined Asian hospitality and set new standards on luxury and what it means to be a host. Today our service excellence is legendary within the hospitality industry. We are proud of our Asian heritage and strive to become the best-loved hospitality group bringing people together to live, work, play, eat and rest well.

Ours is a story driven by an enduring passion for customer service. From pioneering the concept of Asian hospitality, we have radically changed the face of Asia's urban landscape and influenced the way people connect and engage with each other.
