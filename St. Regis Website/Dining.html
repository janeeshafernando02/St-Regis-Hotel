Unique Dining

Unlocking a new social and dining scene in the city

Immersed in the heart of Colombo's vibrant dining and entertainment district, the six restaurants and three bars at St.Regis Colombo offer an uncompromising selection of local and international cuisine, al fresco dining, live entertainment, innovative menus and stylish venues to celebrate exquisite culinary flavours. Discover traditional Chinese cuisine at our internationally recognized signature restaurant St. Regis Palace, feast at the city's most authentic grill & steakhouse at Capital Bar & Grill or revel in the flavours and aromas at Central where we bring to life an elevated buffet dining experience with 16 live stations serving made-to-order cuisine. Dine in good company in our private and semi-private dining rooms, enjoying tasteful authenticity in an intimate setting.
